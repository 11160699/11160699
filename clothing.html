Smart clothes are traditional clothing items that contain modern technology. Some feature meshed wiring woven into the fabric, while others contain hardware that connects via Bluetooth to an iPhone or Android smartphone. Clothing such as Bluetooth beanies have been around for years, but newer examples of smart clothing expand on technology.
